What Are You Looking For?

Find answers to common questions about our cloud surveillance system, including setup, features, storage, security, and compatibility. Get the information you need with ease.

What is Quardzlahnn’s Cloud Video Surveillance?

Quardzlahnn offers an AI-powered cloud surveillance system that provides real-time monitoring, secure cloud storage, and advanced analytics for enhanced security management and remote access.

How does the Cloud Adapter work?

Our Cloud Adapter seamlessly integrates with existing cameras and recorders, enabling cloud storage and remote access without complicated network configurations or hardware upgrades.

What are the storage options?

We support up to 8MP video recording with flexible cloud storage plans, allowing footage to be retained for durations ranging from a few days to several years.

How does the Surveillance Command Centre help?

It acts as a centralized hub for security operations, providing real-time monitoring, AI-driven threat detection, and instant alerts to improve incident response.

Why choose cloud-based surveillance over traditional CCTV?

Cloud-based surveillance eliminates bulky hardware, reduces maintenance costs, provides remote access to footage, and offers scalability without requiring major infrastructure upgrades.

Can the system prevent theft and vandalism?

Yes, our system uses live monitoring, motion detection, and AI-based alerts to detect suspicious activities in real-time and take proactive security measures.

Is it compatible with my existing setup?

Yes, our Cloud Adapter integrates effortlessly with both new and legacy surveillance systems, enhancing security without the need for new camera installations.

How is my data protected?

We ensure data security with encrypted cloud storage, multi-layer authentication, and strict privacy policies to safeguard your surveillance footage from unauthorized access.

How do I get started?

You can start with our 7-day free trial, which allows you to experience our cloud surveillance features before committing to a subscription plan.

How can I contact support?

You can reach us through our ‘Contact Us’ page, where we provide email, phone, and chat support for any inquiries or technical assistance.
